Typographic Cookbook

For this assignment, my recipe book focuses on vegan recipes for young adults. The primary goal was to create a visually appealing book that showcases simple yet delicious vegan recipes. The vibrant colors reflect the "rainbow diet," inspired by fruits, vegetables, and plant-based food. The book merges typography, engaging visuals, and easy-to-follow recipes, appealing to both vegans and non-vegans.
