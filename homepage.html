Typographic cookbook
For this assignment , my recipe book focuses on vegan recipes for young adults. The primary goal was to create a visually appealing book that showcases simple yet delicious vegan recipes as there is an increase in popularity of plant-based. The book is inclusive, catering not only to vegans but also to anyone interested in healthy and wholesome meals. The colour scheme of the book revolves around the "rainbow diet," inspired by the vibrant colors of plant-based foods. Vegan food is often associated with an array of colorful fruits, vegetables, and grains, symbolizing health and vitality. The vibrant scheme also resonates with the youthful energy of the target audience. Through this project, I explored how typography and design can elevate the experience of using a recipe book, by merging vibrant colors, clean layouts, and a purposeful font pairing, The Vegan Fix offers an accessible and inspiring resource for young adults exploring the world of vegan cooking.
